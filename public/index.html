About Code and Mushroom Learning

Mushroom Learning started in Austin, TX as a subset of MakerSquare's adult coding program. From promotional workshops to longitudinal courses, Mushroom Learning was recently recognized by Google and code.org for delivering coding education to local public, private, and charter schools.

Workshops

Summer Camp

This is the morel of our mushroom medley. Mushroom Learnings summer camp is a fun way to learn web design in an immersive educational environment and enjoy the summer with friends. In addition to coding, our camp includes fun unplugged games and artificial intelligence activities.

Learn more »

THE MUSHROOM PROGRAM

This is program is the full Shiitake. Our 8-week program covers coding languages html, css, and javascript in an our proprietary learning environment. In addition to our web design course, we will be offering a Minecraft modding course that combines JavaScript with the fun of gaming.

Learn more!

Pair Programming

Our mentors will help your student design and execute on a web project. From scoping out the initial idea, to writing specifications, and finally producing the final product, the mentors will give individualized attention to any of the ideas that mushroom out of your imagination.
