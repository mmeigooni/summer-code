Why Code?

Mushroom Learning started in Austin, TX as a subset of MakerSquare's adult coding program. From promotional workshops to longitudinal courses, Mushroom Learning was recently recognized by Google and code.org for delivering coding education to local public, private, and charter schools.

Why Code?

Computer science develops studentsâ€™ computational and critical thinking skills and shows them how to create, not simply use, new technologies. Mushroom Learning believes this fundamental knowledge is needed to prepare students for the 21st century, regardless of their ultimate field of study or occupation.

At Mushroom Learning, students have a chance to get introduced to computer science before taking a class at the AP level. Mushroom Learning programs focus on front-end web development languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These languages have a more forgiving syntax and give immediate visual feedback to students. They are also currently the most in demand coding languages in industry. These reasons combine to give Mushroom Learning students a decided edge in computer science.
