Cancellation Policy

Any cancelation more than a week before the course will receive a full refund minus a 5% fee. Any cancellation with less than a week's notice will not be entitled to any refund. Once issued, refunds typically take 5-7 days to post. Refunds will not be issued once a course or private tutoring program begins. In the event of a scheduling conflict, any course enrollment can be moved to another location or rescheduled to a later session without penalty.

In the event that a school or Mushroom Learning cancels a school fundraiser that is not part of a course, all payments for this program are considered a donation to the school and are non-refundable.
