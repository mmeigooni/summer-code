    Do this before next class!

    Pick a category, either books or movies and do the following:

    1. Make a list of your 3 favorites in your category. For example, if I picked books, I would make a list of my 3 favorite books.
    2. Write at least one sentence on why each of the items made it on your favorites list
    3. Using Google image search, find a link to a picture of each of your 3 favorite things

    Once you're done, email sentences and the links of the pictures to review@mushroomlearning.com with the subject line "Homework 1"

    Due before midnight next class!
