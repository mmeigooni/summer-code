FAQ

What are the age requirements on the program?
Most of our students are 12 or over. We occasionally make exceptions for advanced younger students. Please email hello@mushroomlearning.com if you would would like to know more about exceptions.
Is there any any experience required to join?
This class is inteded for students with no previous coding experience. There will be around 2 hours of pre-work to be done before the first day of class.

Who are your instructors?
Our instructors are all coding ninjas that make learning fun. They all are vetted to not only be coding gurus, but have to have fun engaging personalities, you might even have a former Jeopardy contestant teaching one of your classes. What types of projects will students be doing?
Students will learn the basics of web development and we will help guide and scope a different project for every student.
Why take a Mushroom Learning course?

Computational thinking teaches you how to tackle large problems by breaking them down into a sequence of smaller, more manageable problems. It allows you to tackle complex problems in efficient ways that operate at huge scale. It involves creating models of the real world with a suitable level of abstraction, and focus on the most pertinent aspects. It helps you go from specific solutions to general ones.

Computational thinking is a skill that everyone should learn. Even if you never become a professional software engineer, you will benefit from knowing how to think this way. It will help you understand and master technology of all sorts and solve problems in almost any discipline.
