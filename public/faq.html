FAQ

Mushroom Learning -- FAQ

What are the age requirements on the summer camp?
Most of our students are 12 or over. We occasionally make exceptions for advanced younger students. Please email hello@mushroomlearning.com if you would would like to know more about exceptions.
Is there any any experience required to join?
This class is inteded for students with no previous coding experience. There will be around 2 hours of pre-work to be done before the first day of class.
What kind of snacks and lunch will be provided?

Snacks will vary from sliced fruit to hummus and pita chips and lightly salted popcorn. Lunches will be a balanced combination of easy to eat foods.

If you have any concerns or there are any dietary restrictions we should know about, please email hello@mushroomlearning.com.
