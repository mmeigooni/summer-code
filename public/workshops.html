Overview

What is a website?

Want to know what the summer program will be like? Sign up for a free, hands-on workshop that gives students a taste of what the camp has in store for them.

Using Mozilla's X-Ray Goggles, students will learn how the skeleton of a website is structured. By the end of the workshop, students will understand basic HTML tags and also remix a website.
